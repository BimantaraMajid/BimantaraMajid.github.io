Fitures

Users can become content creators, content is divided into two public and premium. Uploaded content can be in the form of photos, videos, text (writing/stories), and any files such as design templates etc.

Users can also become content connoisseurs by following content creators they admire. In addition, you can also donate if you want to appreciate content creators without a minimum donation limit.

This ranking is a feature for content creators as our appreciation to content creators. Ranking is given based on how often you create content, the number of likes and views.

Rewards given in the form of points, both for content lovers and content creators. The points earned can be exchanged for various products in the store menu, or can also be converted into money.
